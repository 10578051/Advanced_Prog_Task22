WELCOME TO JUNE LIVING INTERIORS

Minimalist design meets bold coloring

Founded by Florence June in 1990, June Living is a leader in delivering minimalist interiors blended with bold coloring. As an interior designer, June prides herself on having styled homes for over 10,000 clientele covering a mix of homes throughout Ireland.
Each room can hold a maximum of 2 guests, and has its own en-suite. We pride ourselves on creating beautiful spaces for guests to relax and unwind. We have a communal lounge area where guests can relax and read a newspaper, interact with other guests staying at the lodge or simply sit and enjoy the spectacular views. Breakfast and dinner are provided for guests each evening and there is a communal dining area where guests can enjoy their meals beside the comfort of an open fire. There are still some spaces available to stay in December.
