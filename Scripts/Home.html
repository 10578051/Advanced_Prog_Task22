Welcome to June Living

Looking for minimalist living? Look no further. Here at June Living, we are Ireland's go to minimalist interior design specialists. Specialising in Scandanavian design with an Irish touch, our interior designers have collectively over 50 years experience in designing breathtaking, modern spaces to families and couples all across the breadth of the country.
June Living offers interior design consultancy and operates a shop which sells minimalist, affordable, stylish furniture. Bespoke pieces are created by Irish designers and will be sure to stand out in your home.
June Living was created when interior designers Mary Brown, Derek Lang & Sean Murphy came together and discovered that Ireland was lacking in minimalist interiors which could be designed and purchased at affordable prices for couples and families alike.
